## product description 


- Larger view
With focusing and shooting integrated patented technology and a focusing range from 5mm to infinity, it features 1080P full HD and can realize the imaging of patients' root canals, double teeth, full mouth and face portrait.

- Ultra-low distortion optical lens
The lowest distortion design which is lowers than 5%, restoring the tooth structure more realistically

- Durable metal body
CNC is carefully carved, fashionable and sturdy. By using anodized process, it is durable, not easy to change color, easier to clean and healthier.

- 3D adjustable focus slider
The focus switch and the shooting switch are in the same position, so the doctor does not need to move his finger to complete the shot. Its one-handed focus photography function allows it to be operated with different fingers and hands. Adjustable focus makes it to be more fast and convenient. It’s the DSLR in intraoral cameras.


- Close up dental photography
For patients with limited mouth opening, it is easier to obtain clear images of posterior teeth.

- Root canal microscopy in intraoral cameras
Similar to root canal microscopes, it observes the washing of the root canal wall and the root canal opening after pulp opening. With different field of view and different depth of field and focal length range, you can get more contents with different depths of field when taking the same photo. Therefore, you are able to get clearer images when select the required contents later. The effect of root canal microscopes, the price of intraoral cameras.

- High resolution sensors
Large surface 1/3-inch sensor which is imported from the USA. Single-chip WDR dynamic solution, larger than 115db range, 1080p security dedicated sensor. The obtained hyperspectral image can provide a continuous spectral curve and improve the accuracy of tooth color judgment. Therefore, the colorimetric results are more scientific and reasonable.


https://www.handyimaging.com/uploads/Intraoral-Camera-HDI-712D-9.jpg

- Natural light lighting
6 LED lights distributed around the perimeter of the lens not only allow the lens to obtain the target image with better illumination but also meet the needs of the best light source for tooth colorimetry.

https://www.handyimaging.com/uploads/Intraoral-Camera-HDI-712D-10-121x300.jpg

- UVC Free-Driver
Compliant with standard UVC protocol, it eliminates the tedious process of installing drivers and allows a plug-and-use. As long as the third-party software supports the UVC protocol, it can also be used directly without additional drivers.


- Twain standard protocol
The unique scanner driver protocol of Twain allows our sensors to be perfectly compatible with other software. Therefore, you can still use the existing database and software while using Handy’s sensors, removing your trouble of expensive imported brands’ sensors repair or high-cost replacement.


https://www.handyimaging.com/uploads/HDR-500600-7.jpg

As the digital image management software, HandyDentist, was carefully developed by Handy’s engineers, it only takes 1 minute to install and 3 minutes to get started. It realizes one-click image processing, saves doctors' time to easily finds problems and efficiently completes diagnosis and treatment. HandyDentist image management software provides a powerful management system to facilitate effective communication between doctors and patients.
